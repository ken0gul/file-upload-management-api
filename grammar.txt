 --- The difference between "on one's own and by one's self"
 In summary, "on your own" generally highlights self-reliance and taking responsibility, while "by yourself" focuses on being alone without others. 
 
 => I prefer to eat dinner by myself.(I enjoy dining alone with the company of others)
 => He built the entire business on his own.(He established the business without any help.)
